1 Merchant Integration Guide

Step 1: Get Whitelisted

Contact the Gateway Admin (Jaden) with the following details to activate your site:

  • • Website URL: (e.g., yourshop.github.io)
  • • Payment Mode: (Hybrid / Crypto / Interac)
  • • Receiving Email: (Your alert email)
  • • LTC Address: (Your wallet address)
  • • Redirect URL: (Your success page)

Step 2: Link Your "Buy" Buttons

The gateway pulls the price directly from the URL. Link your buttons like this:

https://flashgroweb.github.io/postest/?amt=[PRICE]

HTML Implementation

<a href="https://flashgroweb.github.io/postest/?amt=25.00" 
   class="button-style">
   Buy Now for $25.00
</a>

Step 3: How You Get Paid

1. Notification

Receive an email with the Customer Email and Transaction ID (Memo) once they click "Complete".

2. Verification

Check your bank or LTC wallet. If the funds match the Memo, fulfill the order!
